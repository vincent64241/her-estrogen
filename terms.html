10. Important Safety Information (Prescription Products)

Estrogen-containing products carry FDA boxed warnings for endometrial cancer; cardiovascular disorders including stroke, heart attack, and venous thromboembolism (deep-vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism); probable dementia; and, in combination with progestins, breast cancer. Use the lowest effective dose for the shortest duration consistent with treatment goals and your individual risk profile.

The medications we coordinate access to are FDA-approved branded or generic estradiol and oral progesterone products. We do not market mass-compounded BHRT. Discuss risks, benefits, contraindications, and alternatives with your clinician before starting treatment.

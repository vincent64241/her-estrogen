Questions? Answered.

Is hormone therapy safe?

For most healthy women under 60 or within 10 years of menopause, FDA-approved hormone therapy is well-studied and considered safe. A licensed clinician reviews your health history to determine whether it's appropriate for you.

Do I need insurance?

No. HerEstrogen is a cash-pay service with transparent pricing, and many treatments are HSA/FSA eligible — no insurance hurdles or surprise bills.

How soon will I hear back?

A clinician who specializes in women's hormones typically reviews your intake within 2 business days. If treatment is appropriate, your prescription ships free and discreet.

What if it's not right for me?

If a clinician determines treatment isn't appropriate, you won't be charged for medication. You can adjust or cancel your plan anytime — there's no long-term commitment.

Which states do you serve?

We work with licensed clinicians and pharmacies across all 50 states. Some products and lab tests carry state-level restrictions, which are noted before checkout.

Questions? Answered.

Is hormone therapy right for everyone?

No — hormone therapy isn't right for everyone. A licensed clinician reviews your health history and discusses the risks and benefits with you before prescribing. Depending on what's appropriate for you, treatment may involve FDA-approved or compounded medications.

Do I need insurance?

No. HerEstrogen is a cash-pay service with transparent pricing, and many treatments are HSA/FSA eligible — no insurance hurdles or surprise bills.

How soon will I hear back?

A clinician who specializes in women's hormones typically reviews your intake within 2 business days. If treatment is appropriate, your prescription ships free.

What if it's not right for me?

If a clinician determines treatment isn't appropriate, you won't be charged for medication. You can adjust or cancel your plan anytime — there's no long-term commitment.

Which states do you serve?

We work with licensed clinicians and pharmacies across all 50 states. Some products and lab tests carry state-level restrictions, which are noted before checkout.

How much does HerEstrogen cost?

Hormone therapy starts at $90/mo — about $3 a day — and your membership (clinician evaluation, unlimited messaging, ongoing check-ins, and free 2-day shipping) is included at no extra cost. GLP-1 and other treatments are priced separately, and you’ll see exact pricing before checkout. No insurance is needed; many treatments are HSA/FSA eligible.

Is HerEstrogen legitimate and safe to use?

HerEstrogen is LegitScript certified and works only with licensed U.S. clinicians and licensed pharmacies. Treatment is provided only after a licensed clinician reviews your health history and determines it’s appropriate for you; a prescription is not guaranteed.

Are the medications FDA-approved?

It depends on what a clinician determines is appropriate. Treatment may involve FDA-approved medications or compounded medications. Compounded medications are prepared by a licensed compounding pharmacy and are not FDA-approved.

How does HerEstrogen work?

It’s 100% online. You complete a short health intake, a licensed clinician reviews it (typically within 2 business days), and if treatment is appropriate, your prescription ships free to your door. Unlimited messaging with your care team is included.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees — you can adjust or cancel your plan anytime. Plans renew automatically unless cancelled at least 72 hours before the renewal date.
